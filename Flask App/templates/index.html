Overview

  • The aim of this project was to answer the question: is there a connection between the location of these retail stores and the value of nearby real estate?

  • Trader Joe's has built a brand on selling niche goods you can't find at other super market chains, and although not considered high end stores are seemingly located up in nicer more affluent neigherhoods much like Whole Foods.

  • Walmart on the other hand is that one stop superstore that prioritizes the variety of cheep goods it sells and the cost savings it brings to its customers and its sotres are found EVERYWHERE but particularily in low income areas or seemingly so.

  • What is the correlation between house location and proximity to either Trader Joes or Walmart?


The Data

  • Realtor.com dataset with poperty value( our Target), number of baths, number of beds, house size, acre size and location information such as address and zip code.

  • Walmart dataset pulled from Kaggle with store locations and addresses infromation for all the stores in the US as of 2016.

  • Trader Joe's dataset also pulled from Kaggle with store locations(address, zip code, city, state, latitude and longitude) accross the US as of 2016.
